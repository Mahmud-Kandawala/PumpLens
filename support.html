Frequently Asked Questions

How does the OCR receipt scanning work? +

Simply take a photo of your gas receipt using the camera feature. Our advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology will automatically scan and extract key information like the total amount, gallons purchased, and station name. The data is processed locally on your device for privacy and security.

Can I track multiple vehicles? +

Yes! PumpLens supports tracking multiple vehicles. You can add cars, trucks, motorcycles, or any vehicle, each with their own separate fuel history and analytics. Switch between vehicles easily and view individual or combined statistics.

Is my data secure and private? +

Absolutely. All your fuel data is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in security features. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

What's included in the premium subscription? +

Premium subscribers get unlimited fuel entries (free users are limited to 5 entries), advanced analytics with detailed charts and trends, data export capabilities, and priority customer support. We offer monthly, yearly, and lifetime subscription options.

Can I export my data? +

Yes, premium users can export all their fuel data to CSV format. This is perfect for tax purposes, expense reports, or creating backups. The export includes all entry details like dates, amounts, gallons, stations, and vehicle information.

How do I restore my purchases? +

Go to the Premium tab in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any previous purchases linked to your Apple ID. If you're still having issues, please contact support.

What iOS version is required? +

PumpLens requires iOS 15.0 or later and is optimized for all iPhone models. The app uses modern iOS features for the best performance and user experience.

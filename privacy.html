1. Introduction

Welcome to PumpLens ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application PumpLens (the "App"). This policy applies to all users of our App and describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

PumpLens is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Key Privacy Principles

  • Local Storage First: Your fuel tracking data is stored locally on your device
  • No Unnecessary Collection: We only collect information essential for app functionality
  • User Control: You maintain full control over your data
  • Transparency: We are clear about what data we collect and why

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using the App:

  • Fuel Entry Data: Gas station names, fuel amounts, prices, dates, odometer readings, and related notes you enter
  • Vehicle Information: Car make, model, year, and custom names you assign to your vehicles
  • Receipt Images: Photos of gas receipts you choose to capture for OCR processing (processed locally)
  • User Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, and customization choices
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting customer support

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: App crashes, performance metrics, and feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase receipts and subscription status (through Apple's StoreKit)

2.3 Camera and Photo Access

The App requests camera access for:

  • Taking photos of gas receipts for OCR processing
  • Capturing odometer readings
  • All image processing occurs locally on your device
  • Images are stored locally and never transmitted to external servers

Important Note About OCR Processing

When you use our OCR (Optical Character Recognition) feature, receipt images are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No images or extracted text are sent to external servers or third-party OCR services.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Core App Functionality: Storing and organizing your fuel tracking data
  • Analytics and Insights: Calculating fuel efficiency, costs, and trends
  • Data Export: Generating CSV files for your personal use
  • Reminders: Sending local notifications based on your preferences
  • Multi-Vehicle Support: Managing separate tracking for different vehicles

3.2 App Improvement

  • Performance Optimization: Using anonymized usage data to improve app performance
  • Feature Development: Understanding which features are most valuable to users
  • Bug Fixes: Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • User Experience: Enhancing the overall app experience

3.3 Customer Support

  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Providing assistance with app features
  • Processing subscription and billing inquiries

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

PumpLens prioritizes local data storage:

  • On-Device Storage: All fuel tracking data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms
  • iOS Data Protection: Your data is protected by iOS's built-in encryption when your device is locked
  • App Sandbox: Data is isolated within the app's secure sandbox environment
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your fuel tracking data on external servers

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Data Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using iOS's built-in encryption
  • Secure Transmission: Any network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls for any backend services
  • Regular Security Reviews: Ongoing security assessments and updates

4.3 Data Backup and Sync

  • iCloud Backup: Your data may be included in iOS device backups to iCloud (controlled by your iOS settings)
  • Device Transfer: Data transfers between devices occur through iOS's standard backup/restore process
  • Manual Export: You can export your data to CSV format for personal backup

Data Security Commitment

We are committed to protecting your information using appropriate technical and organizational measures. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 General Policy

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your fuel tracking data remains private and is not shared with external parties.

5.2 Limited Exceptions

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Safety Concerns: To protect the safety of users or the public
  • Rights Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with user notification)

5.3 Service Providers

We may work with trusted third-party service providers for:

  • App Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics (no personal data)
  • Crash Reporting: Technical error reporting (no personal data)
  • Payment Processing: Apple's App Store for in-app purchases
  • Customer Support: Email services for support communications

Third-Party Service Providers

Any third-party service providers we work with are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the specific services they provide to us. They are prohibited from using your information for their own purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your fuel tracking data within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update your fuel entries and vehicle information
  • Deletion: Delete individual entries or all data from the app
  • Export: Export your data to CSV format (premium feature)

6.2 App Permissions

You can control app permissions through iOS Settings:

  • Camera Access: Required for receipt scanning and photo capture
  • Notifications: Control reminder notifications
  • Photo Library: Access to save or import images (if applicable)

6.3 Marketing Communications

  • We do not send promotional emails or marketing communications
  • App Store notifications are controlled by your iOS settings
  • In-app notifications can be disabled in the app settings

6.4 Data Deletion

To completely remove your data:

  1. Delete the app from your device
  2. This will permanently remove all locally stored fuel tracking data
  3. Contact us if you need assistance with data deletion

7. Children's Privacy

PumpLens is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7.1 Age Verification

  • The app is rated 4+ on the App Store but is designed for users who can legally drive
  • We rely on Apple's App Store age verification processes
  • Users must be at least 13 years old to create accounts or use the app

7.2 Parental Rights

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13:

  • We will delete that information from our records
  • We will not use the information for any purpose
  • We will not disclose the information to third parties
